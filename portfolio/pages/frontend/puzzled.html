Goals

When making this website my main goal was to make some very fun puzzles and share it with my friends, and this shaped the full structure of the site. I ended up making the top priority as the ease of sharing the puzzles with others and having fun, because you don't like the puzzle until you know the answer and you share it with your friend just to see them struggle. I made the site very playful with vibrant colors depicting the playfulness. I created some nasty puzzles with JavaScript, see if you can solve the medium difficulty one :).
