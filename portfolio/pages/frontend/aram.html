Goals

The goals of Aram Visai as an NGO were to provide the poor and the needy people with free resources, job offers and education with the help of students. Aram stands for students Visai stands for force so as a whole it represents a platform where students can also be a part by sharing their knowledge and prowess. The main design goals when designing the the website were, to get more donations as a priority, easyness to apply for being a volunteer or a speaker and being a bit mordern with India being the center focus throughout the design.

Typography

Montserrat font was chosen as a way of representing the modernness, and student driven nature
