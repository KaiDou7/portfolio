Goals

The main goals when creating this website was the ability of the design and function to shout out luxury, authenticity and modern. And so to show this I decided to go for a very simple but eye grabbing design. The reason for such priorities was the brand/product itself, as it sold wedding dresses they had to be luxurious and authentic for that special day in every women's life as well as not outdated.
