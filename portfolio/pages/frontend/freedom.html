Goals

The main goal of the project was to create a travel agency site where even if you went with no intention of travelling, before leaving the site the travel instinct starts building into you and it ends up becoming irresistible to travel. What started as, simply a way of testing if I am capable of making animations on scroll with pure Vanilla JavaScript ended up growing onto me and becoming the thing it is now.

Typography

Recoleta font was used to signify the boldness and trustfullness of the Freedom.co travelling Agency
